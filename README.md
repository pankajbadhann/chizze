# Chizze

> Most software stores information.
> Chizze preserves context.

Chizze is an open-source Context Operating System that helps individuals and teams connect conversations, goals, decisions, relationships, and knowledge into actionable understanding.

Instead of treating chats, notes, plans, and goals as separate things, Chizze keeps them connected so users can understand not only **what happened**, but also **why it happened**, **what it means**, and **what should happen next**.

---

## Why Chizze Exists

Modern life is fragmented.

A single goal may involve:

* WhatsApp conversations
* Meeting discussions
* Notes
* Decisions
* Plans
* Tasks
* Relationships

These pieces live in different places.

As time passes, context is lost.

People remember outcomes but forget:

* why decisions were made
* what discussions led to them
* who was involved
* what changed along the way

Chizze exists to preserve that missing layer.

---

## The Problem

Today's tools optimize for one thing:

| Tool Type      | Optimizes For       |
| -------------- | ------------------- |
| Messaging Apps | Communication       |
| Note Apps      | Information Storage |
| Task Managers  | Execution           |
| Calendars      | Scheduling          |
| AI Assistants  | Answers             |

The missing layer is:

**Context**

The connection between:

* conversations
* goals
* decisions
* actions
* people

---

## What Chizze Does

Chizze helps users:

### Preserve Context

Keep important conversations, ideas, decisions, and goals connected.

### Build Understanding

Understand relationships between events, discussions, and outcomes.

### Improve Execution

Convert context into meaningful actions and recommendations.

### Maintain Continuity

Track the evolution of goals, projects, teams, and personal growth over time.

---

## Core Concepts

### People

Individuals and collaborators connected to your journey.

### Conversations

Discussions that create context and influence decisions.

### Goals

Desired outcomes and long-term intentions.

### Decisions

Important commitments and conclusions.

### Context

The information surrounding everything above.

---

## Product Vision

We believe people do not suffer from a lack of information.

They suffer from a lack of connected context.

Chizze aims to become a Context Operating System where:

```text
People
↓
Conversations
↓
Goals
↓
Decisions
↓
Actions
↓
Progress
```

remain connected over time.
